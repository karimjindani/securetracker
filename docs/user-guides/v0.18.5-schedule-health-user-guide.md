# v0.18.5 Schedule Health User Guide

## Dashboard

The Dashboard shows three schedule-health groups:

- **On Track**: engagement is not currently at schedule risk.
- **Needs Attention**: planned or active engagement is within the 7-day warning window.
- **At Risk**: planned end date has expired and the engagement is not closed, Go-Live, or cancelled.

Use **View Needs Attention** or **View At Risk** to open the Engagements Kanban with the matching filter applied.

## Engagements Kanban

The Engagements page includes a **Schedule health** filter. Cards show a colored health chip when schedule health applies.

Schedule health is computed dynamically from planned dates and current lifecycle status; it is not a manually edited field.
