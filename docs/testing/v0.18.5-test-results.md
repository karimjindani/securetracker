## Covered Scenarios

- Future planned engagements classify as On Track.
- Planned or active engagements near their planned dates classify as Needs Attention.
- Non-terminal engagements past planned end date classify as At Risk.
- Terminal engagements are excluded from attention/risk counts.
- Dashboard links open filtered Engagements Kanban views.
- `GET /dashboard/summary` exposes top-level schedule-health counts and attention/risk short lists.
- `GET /engagements?scheduleHealth=YELLOW|RED` returns only matching derived schedule-health rows.
